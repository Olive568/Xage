What is the purpose of this website?

The website allows users to create and sell customized products like shirts, mugs, and more. We handle the production process, offering a range of suppliers, materials, and delivery options.

What types of products can I create and sell on the website?

You can create and sell a variety of products such as T-shirts, mugs, phone cases, posters, and other customizable items.

How does the filter mode work?

The filter mode allows you to narrow down your choices by material, location, price range, and other factors. The system then recommends the best suppliers based on your preferences.

Can I choose my own suppliers for the products?

Yes, you can choose from a list of recommended suppliers for each part of the production process, such as the product itself, design, and delivery.

Who handles the production of the customized items?

We handle the production with the help of your chosen suppliers. You focus on the design and customization, while we take care of the rest.

Is it possible to sell my own designs on this app?

Absolutely! You can upload your designs, select the product and material, and our suppliers will create the items based on your specifications.

How do I know which supplier to choose for my product?

Our system automatically recommends the best suppliers based on factors like material quality, location, price, and user reviews.

Can I change the supplier after placing an order?

Once an order is confirmed and production begins, it might not be possible to change the supplier. We recommend carefully reviewing your choices before finalizing.
